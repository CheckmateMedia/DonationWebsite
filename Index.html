Welcome to We Just Keep on Swimming

We are a nonprofit dedicated to supporting survivors of traumatic brain injuries. The founder of We Just Keep on Swimming is also a victim of a severe traumatic brain injury who intends to help to make life easier for other survivors with this nonprofit organization.

Please support Matthew Brignola, the founder, to help survivors.

Incurring a brain inbjury is a tragedy, which is perhaps why some incur what is actually called a traumatic bran injury. You have the power to help us make a difference. Please help donate to our nonprofit so we can help survivors by raising money to help pay for their extensive medical bills. Your support or donations would be greatly appreciated. We teach survivors how to code websites so that they aquire a new skill in order to earn money themselves. Most states require you to be cleared by a doctor before you can work if you have a tbi(traumatic brain injury), which in Matt's case means that you may lose a way to earn money on your own for many months if not years. Matthew was an extremely hard worker and his injury's medical costs depleted his bank account. If you are interested in having a website made please go here. Choosing us would satisfy your need for a website and help to bring survivors back into the working world. Matthew has written an autobiographical novel titled We Just Keep on Swimming that reflects mainly on his recovery from his traumatic brain injury.
