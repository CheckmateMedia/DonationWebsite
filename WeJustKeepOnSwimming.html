Matthew Brignola wrote this autobiographical novel that reflects heavily on his traumatic brain injury. Matthew wants this novel to
communicate that one with a braininjury may still accomplish his or her dreams. Matthew had wanted to write a novel since before
he had a traumatic brain injury. Hopefully that is evidence that one can still achieve his or her goals and dreams despite having a brain injury.
